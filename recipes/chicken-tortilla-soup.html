A Spicy and Simple Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe

A simple, 11 ingredient chicken tortilla soup recipe with some kick

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 onion roughly chopped
  • 3 cloves garic
  • 1 14 oz can fire roasted tomatoes
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1 jalapeno stem and seeds removed
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Sour cream for finishing

Instructions

  1. Heat a saucepan over medium heat and add olive oil
  2. Saute onion, garlic, and jalapeno until onions become translucent
  3. Add tomoatoes and stock and simmer for 30 minutes
  4. Use an immersion blender and blend until smooth
  5. Add black beans, corn, and chicken and heat for 5 minutes
  6. Serve with cheese and sour cream on top
