Barbecue Chicken Pizza Recipe

A tasty and wholly common recipe for a barbecue chicken pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. store-bought pizza dough
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 1 tbsp cornmeal
  • 2/3 cup your favourite barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup shredded chicken
  • 2/3 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 2/3 cup smoked gouda cheese
  • 1/2 small red onion
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475 F
  2. Divide dough in half and freeze the rest for another time
  3. Lightly flour a work surface
  4. Gently flatten and shape the dough into a disc
  5. Place dough on a pan and work it into a 12-inch circle, about 1/2 inch thick
  6. Once shaped pinch the edges to create a rim
  7. Cover and allow to rest while preparing the res of the ingredients
  8. Brush olive oil all over the dough
  9. Spread 1/2 of your barbecue sauce evenly on top of the dough
  10. Toss the chicken with the remaining sauce, then spread it evenly on the dough
  11. Top the dough evenly with cheese and onion
  12. Bake 14-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown
  13. Optional: Sprinkle with cliantro if desired
  14. Slice hot pizza and serve immediately
